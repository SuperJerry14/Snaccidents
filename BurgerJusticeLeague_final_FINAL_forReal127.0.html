📜 League Lore

  • 🕷 Arachnid Lad: Bitten by a radioactive wrapper behind a dumpster at Burger Boy HQ. Can swing between fryers using ketchup lines and smells permanently of onion rings. Fights crime and clogged grease traps with equal enthusiasm.
  • ⚡ Thore: Demigod of scattered thunderstorms and ergonomic mattress discounts. Hurls lightning patties, brings rain to dry buns, and moonlights as a chiropractor for Norse mascots. Loves protein shakes and Norse mythology TikToks.
  • 🛠 Irony Man: Billion-dollar startup bro turned vigilante after his app to rate fast food workers got 1-star bombed. Built his armor from recycled Yelp reviews and sarcasm. Speaks only in quotes from disgruntled customers.
  • 🚚 Optimus Subprime: Cybertruck dropout turned taco-truck overlord. Transforms into a food truck that only plays 80s synthwave. Leader of the Cravetrons, he delivers spicy justice in 30 minutes or less or vengeance is free.
  • 🦖 Compy Comrade: Tiny dinosaur, massive manifesto. Founded the “United Fryworkers of Pangea.” Believes all burgers belong to the proletariat, but keeps a private stash of golden patties for the Central Grill Committee.
  • ☠️ The Purple Inconvenience: A misunderstood intern from the Multiverse of Meal Preppers. Claims he was “inevitably correct” about portion control. Snapped half the fries out of existence because the drive-thru line was too long.
